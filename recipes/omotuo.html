Recipe for Fufu

h2>Description

Today we are going to summarize the steps involved in making Omotuo. You can never go wrong with this

Ingredients

  1. Cassava
  2. Plantain
  3. Chicken
  4. Garlic
  5. Ginger
  6. Pepper
  7. Tomatoes
  8. Onions
  9. Tomato Paste
  10. Chicken Spices

Steps

Omotuo is very easy to prepare. Basically you have to start by doing the following:

  1. Peel the cassava and plantain
  2. Bring it to a boil
  3. Pound and mix it up
  4. Wash and steam your chicken
  5. Add tomato paste and chicken spices to the chicken
  6. Add salt
  7. Blend ginger, garlic, onions, and pepper
  8. Boil tomatoes and onions
  9. Add blended mixture to chicken
  10. Blend tomatoes and onions, add some more pepper
  11. Add that to the chicken
  12. Allow it to heat for a while and later simmer down
  13. Serve with the fufu
  14. Enjoy it

Once you follow these steps, you should be good to go
